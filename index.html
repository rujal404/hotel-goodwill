Welcome to our Luxury Boutique Hotel

Experience one of the
most beautiful hotels in a different,
and intensely personal way.

Step into the serene world of Hotel Goodwill, a cherished heritage boutique hotel in the vibrant city of Patan. Just moments away from revered world heritage sites, our hotel is a tranquil haven for global travelers. Adorned with Patan's cultural treasures, from traditional statues to terracotta masterpieces, Hotel Goodwill is your gateway to Nepalese artistry. Embrace the comfort of home in our peaceful retreat, where every stay is a discovery of "The Hidden Treasure" of Nepal.
